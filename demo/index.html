Objective:

Grow: Cellular automata is trained to achieve a reconstruction target within a number of iterations—but it doesn't know what to do after...

Persist: Cellular automata is trained to reconstruct the target from various starting states—including fully-reconstructed ones.

Regenerate: Similar to the 'persist' objective, however the samples from the pool are exposed to damage, allowing the model to learn to "regrow" damaged cells.
